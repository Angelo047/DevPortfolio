Hydroponic pH Monitoring System

The Hydroponic pH Monitoring System is an innovative solution designed to automate pH monitoring and adjustment in hydroponic farming, ensuring optimal nutrient absorption for plant growth. This project combines hardware and software to measure and regulate the pH levels in hydroponic nutrient solutions, reducing the need for manual checks and adjustments.

Equipped with pH sensors and automated dosing mechanisms, the system continuously monitors pH levels and makes adjustments as needed to maintain a stable environment. The real-time data is displayed on a user-friendly dashboard, allowing users to monitor their system remotely and receive alerts when critical changes occur.

By automating the pH adjustment process, this system significantly reduces labor and improves efficiency, making it ideal for both small-scale and commercial hydroponic setups. Its precision ensures that plants receive the optimal nutrient balance, contributing to higher yield and better quality crops.
