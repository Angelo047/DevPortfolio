Online Servicing System for Efficient Document Requests

The Online Servicing System is designed to streamline and enhance the process of document requests within Barangay Gulod. This web-based application provides residents with a convenient platform to submit requests for various documents, such as certificates, permits, and other official papers, without the need for physical visits to the barangay hall.

Users can easily create an account, log in, and access a user-friendly interface to fill out their requests. The system allows for the attachment of necessary documents and provides real-time status updates on the progress of their requests. Notifications are sent to users via email or SMS, ensuring they are informed throughout the process.

This system significantly reduces wait times and enhances the efficiency of service delivery in Barangay Gulod. By digitizing the request process, it minimizes paperwork, improves record-keeping, and fosters better communication between the barangay officials and the community. This innovative solution aims to promote transparency and accessibility in local governance.
