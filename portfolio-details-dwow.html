DWOW Portal

The DWOW Portal is a dynamic web application designed to streamline employee recognition and reward management. Built using ASP.NET MVC, C#, and HANA Studio, the platform enables companies to allocate points to employees based on their achievements, encouraging a culture of continuous improvement and engagement within the organization.

Through an intuitive interface, managers can easily assign points for various achievements, and employees can monitor their progress. Points can be redeemed for rewards, supporting a seamless rewards process and reducing administrative effort.

Key features include real-time tracking of employee achievements, automated point allocation, and a reward redemption system. This solution not only motivates employees but also simplifies recognition management, allowing companies to focus more on fostering a positive workplace culture.
