About me

I grew up with a curiosity about how things work, often tinkering with gadgets and figuring out ways to make them more efficient or adaptable. This curiosity led me to pursue Information Technology, where I discovered my passion for problem-solving and building systems that serve practical needs. In high school and college, I was drawn to projects that allowed me to create solutions from scratch. One of my proudest projects was a hydroponic pH monitoring system, a capstone that blended software and hardware to automate pH adjustments. It helped me see how technology could drive real-world impact.

Professionally, I dove into SAP implementation and web development internships, which equipped me with hands-on experience and a broader understanding of how technology supports businesses. My role as a Technical Consultant at Direc Business Technology Inc. gave me the chance to work directly with clients, understand their needs, and implement customized solutions. I realized that my favorite part of the job was translating client requirements into technical outcomes that were practical and efficient.

Now, I’m driven to expand my skills further and contribute to projects that blend innovation with user-focused design. I look forward to a career where I can continue solving challenges, developing systems, and making technology more accessible and impactful.
